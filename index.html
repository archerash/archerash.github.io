NRun - Simple init system for Unix/Linux systems

NRun is simple init system written in C that is capable of mounting drives, running services, cleaning up zombie processes and shutting down. You can view its source here. It is still not finished and I do not recommend running it on your own OS. I do NOT provide any kind of warranty for this software.
